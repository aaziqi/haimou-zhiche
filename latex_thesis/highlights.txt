Multi-perceptual (MP) constraints are introduced to stabilize unpaired underwater image enhancement.
MP combines complementary cues: a gray-world color prior, gradient-based structure consistency, and VGG perceptual consistency.
The proposed training-time constraints do not increase inference-time complexity.
Unified in-domain and cross-domain evaluations on EUVP and UIEB support small but consistent gains and competitive generalization tendencies.
